Project TRACE — How it works, end to end

This document serves as the complete operational manual for Project TRACE. It maps the exact digital workflow to the physical realities of the PLP Registrar and Finance Offices, utilizing our new high-density Command-Center UI.

Core Operational Principles

Maximized Screen Reality

Clerks use full-width, fluid dashboards. Data is dense but legible, ensuring staff can monitor active queues, pending scans, and KPI metrics without endless scrolling.

Secure Finance Handoffs

All online payments require manual cross-referencing by the Finance Clerk using uploaded GCash receipts to ensure perfect accounting accuracy before routing to the Secretary.

Automation over Manual Entry

Clerks are decision-makers, not data encoders. The EasyOCR engine extracts text from Window 1 physical papers and presents it in a split-screen verification modal for fast approval.

1. The Interactive Role Gateway

TRACE utilizes a dynamic, role-based login screen. Choosing a persona completely alters the available navigation sidebars and workspace layout.

  • Student: Accesses the unified request form and tracking grid.
  • Finance Clerk: Accesses the Pending Payments queue to manually verify GCash Reference numbers.
  • Window 1 Clerk: Accesses the Intake Scanner drop-zone and Release Queue.
  • College Secretary: Accesses the Evaluation Queue and Split-Screen validation tools.
  • Registrar Admin: Accesses ML Forecast charts, AI prescriptive insights, and the student registration verification queue.

2. The 3-Column Student Portal

This is the citizen-facing side of the application. It is designed to be highly aesthetic and informative.

  • Live KPI Row: Students instantly see cards tracking their "Total Requests", "In Progress", and "Completed" documents.
  • Visual Tracker: The main table features dynamic percentage progress bars (color-coded based on completion) rather than generic text statuses.

3. Finance Office Payment Verification

TRACE does not use automated payment gateways to ensure strict compliance with school accounting policies.

  • Student Action: Students scan a static official PLP GCash QR code. They must then upload a screenshot of their receipt and type in the Reference Number into TRACE.
  • Clerk Action: The Finance Clerk opens their dashboard, clicks the pending request, views the screenshot, and checks the official GCash phone to ensure the funds actually arrived. Clicking "Verify & Approve" automatically forwards the request to the Secretary.

4. Command-Center Clerk Workspaces

Traditional physical logbooks are replaced by wide, data-rich digital command centers.

  • Prominent Branding: All staff dashboards feature a bold TRACE header and user-profile avatar, ensuring institutional consistency.
  • KPI Headers: Window 1 and the Secretary constantly see live metrics (e.g., "Pending Evaluation") pinned to the top of their workspace to gauge current backlog levels.

5. Split-Screen OCR Evaluation

Data entry is the slowest part of administrative work. TRACE eliminates it using an evaluation modal.

  • The Process: When the Secretary clicks a document in their queue, a modal expands. On the left is the physical scan. On the right are the extracted data fields.
  • Approval: If correct, the Secretary simply clicks "Approve & Route to Window 1", which automatically triggers the backend to update the student's progress.

6. Predictive Analytics & Admin Tools

This feature upgrades the system from a simple tracking tool into an intelligent management assistant for the Chief Registrar.

  • 7-Day Volume Forecast: A visual chart utilizing Prophet ML predicts the exact incoming document volume for the week.
  • AI Insights (Random Forest): A dark-themed alert panel generates plain-English recommendations based on the forecast, such as advising the admin to reassign staff to Window 1 ahead of a predicted Thursday rush.
  • Student Account Verification Desk: Displays pending registrations where ID/diploma proof failed automatic EasyOCR checks, allowing the Admin to manually review and approve the student.

7. Frequently Asked Questions (Staff)

  • How do students pay if we don't have a payment gateway?

    The system displays the official PLP Finance GCash QR code. The student pays normally through their GCash app, takes a screenshot of the receipt, and uploads that screenshot along with the Reference Number directly into their TRACE request.

  • What if the student inputs a fake GCash Reference Number?

    The request will go to the Finance Clerk's dashboard first. If the clerk checks the official logs and sees the money never arrived, they simply click "Reject" on the dashboard. The student will be notified and the request will be halted.

  • What if the AI OCR reads a student's name or ID incorrectly?

    The AI is highly accurate, but not perfect. This is why the College Secretary uses the Split-Screen Evaluation modal. If the extracted data on the right doesn't match the image on the left, the Secretary can simply click into the text box, type the correct information, and then click "Approve".

Operations GuideProject Rollout & Implementation

Deploying the TRACE Command-Center requires a structured rollout to ensure staff adapt to the new full-width interfaces.

  1. Phase 1: UI Familiarization & Silent Pilot

    Configure the system and train staff on the split-screen modal and manual payment verification.

    • Database Configuration: Input the Window 1 Clerk, the Finance Clerk, and the College Secretary into the system.
    • The Silent Run: For one week, Window 1 uploads physical papers into the new drop-zone to ensure the AI and the digital hand-offs work perfectly.

  2. Phase 2: The Public Student Launch

    Open the beautiful 3-column portal to the student body.

    • Campus Announcement: The university officially announces the TRACE Student Portal.
    • Transitioning Walk-ins: Guide physical walk-in students to use the portal to submit their requirements and upload their GCash receipts.

  3. Phase 3: Activation of AI & Analytics

    With the system fully running, rely entirely on the AI engines to streamline efficiency.

    • Clerks officially stop manually typing student details, relying completely on the Split-Screen EasyOCR verification modal.
    • The Chief Registrar begins checking the "Predictive Analytics Dashboard" every Monday morning.
